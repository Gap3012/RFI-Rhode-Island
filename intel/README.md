# Intel

Drop your research and notes here. All agents check this directory.

## What goes here

- **Meeting notes** — calls or meetings with the agency, incumbent, or partners
- **OSINT findings** — your own web research on the client, competitors, or market
- **Insider knowledge** — political dynamics, decision-maker preferences, budget context
- **Pricing intel** — rate benchmarks, incumbent pricing, cost constraints
- **Staffing notes** — key personnel availability, teaming partner capabilities
- **Bid/no-bid reasoning** — why we're pursuing this, what makes us competitive
- **Anything else** you want the agents to factor into the proposal

## Format

Markdown is ideal but anything readable works (`.md`, `.txt`, `.docx`, `.pdf`).
Name files descriptively — e.g., `2025-06-15-client-call.md`, `competitor-research.md`.
