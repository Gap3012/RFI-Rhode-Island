\subsection{Question 1(b): Driver Recruitment Timeline}
\textit{``We are aware of the national driver shortage. Does your company or organization feel like the above timeline allows for the necessary time to recruit the number of drivers needed per what is currently outlined in this scope?''}

\vspace{0.3cm}

\placeholder{Address whether the timeline allows sufficient time for driver recruitment given the national driver shortage.}

\begin{description}[style=nextline, leftmargin=0.5cm]
    \item[\faIcon{users} \textbf{Scale of Recruitment Challenge:}] \placeholder{RIDE's STS currently requires approximately 334 drivers plus substitutes. If a new operator wins one or more zones, they may need to recruit 50--150+ CDL-endorsed school bus drivers within 6--8 months. In the current labor market, this is extremely challenging.}

    \item[\faIcon{clock} \textbf{CDL Training Timeline:}] \placeholder{A new school bus driver typically requires 6--10 weeks from initial application to road-ready status (background check, CDL training, school bus endorsement, behind-the-wheel training, route familiarization). This means recruitment must begin no later than March 2027 to have drivers ready by July.}

    \item[\faIcon{lightbulb} \textbf{Sentry's Recommendation:}] \placeholder{Allow pre-award recruitment activities. Permit short-listed vendors to begin driver recruitment (at their own risk/cost) before final award, so the pipeline is warm when the contract is executed. Sentry's recruitment technology platform can support this parallel process.}
\end{description}

\placeholder{Address how the timeline affects vehicle procurement. Frame from Sentry's technology perspective:}

\begin{description}[style=nextline, leftmargin=0.5cm]
    \item[\faIcon{bus} \textbf{Vehicle Procurement Lead Times:}] \placeholder{New school bus delivery currently requires 6--12 months from order. An 8-month transition period may not allow sufficient time for operators to order and receive new vehicles, particularly wheelchair-accessible Type A buses with custom modifications.}

    \item[\faIcon{video} \textbf{Video System Installation:}] \placeholder{The July 1, 2027 mandate requires all new buses to have live digital video violation detection systems. Sentry's camera systems can be installed in 1--2 days per vehicle, but installation must be scheduled after vehicle delivery. A compressed timeline increases the risk of buses entering service without cameras installed.}

    \item[\faIcon{recycle} \textbf{Fleet Transition Strategy:}] \placeholder{Sentry recommends RIDE require outgoing operators to make vehicles available for purchase or lease by incoming operators, reducing the need for new vehicle procurement during the transition period.}
\end{description}
